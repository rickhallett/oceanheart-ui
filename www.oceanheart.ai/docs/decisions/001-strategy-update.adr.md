# Course Strategy Update Summary

## What Changed and Why

This document summarizes the strategic refinements made to The Kaishin Method course ecosystem based on `/docs/specs/course-generation-reframe.md`.

---

## KEY STRATEGIC CHANGES

### 1. **30-Day Integration Challenge Becomes Primary Entry Point**

**BEFORE:**
- Multiple entry points with unclear hierarchy
- Foundations course (£97) was primary intro
- 30-Day Challenge (£47) was a side offer

**AFTER:**
- **30-Day Integration Challenge (£47)** is THE primary entry point
- Acts as lead generator and qualifier for 90-Day Transformation
- Clear funnel: Lead Magnet → 30-Day Challenge → 90-Day Transformation

**WHY:**
- Lower risk for customers (£47 vs £97)
- Proves the method works before bigger commitment
- Creates natural progression to flagship offering
- Better conversion funnel economics

---

### 2. **90-Day Transformation Elevated to Flagship Status**

**BEFORE:**
- 90-Day course mentioned but not emphasized
- Positioned as one of many offerings
- No clear "flagship" product

**AFTER:**
- **90-Day Transformation (£497)** is THE flagship offering
- Hero sections emphasize this as the main transformation vehicle
- Clear promise: Circle 1 → Circle 3 in 90 days
- Primary revenue driver

**WHY:**
- Creates clear value proposition (comprehensive transformation)
- Higher price point = better unit economics
- Graduates of 30-Day Challenge naturally upsell to this
- Establishes authority (flagship = serious transformation)

---

### 3. **Clearer Tier Structure**

**BEFORE:**
- Tier 1: £47-97 (multiple options)
- Tier 2: £297-497 (mixed offerings)
- Unclear progression

**AFTER:**
- **Tier 1:** 30-Day Challenge ONLY (£47) - The entry point
- **Tier 2:** 90-Day Transformation (£497), AI Course (£297), Deepening Courses (£497-597) - Immersion
- **Tier 3:** Applied courses (£997+) - Mastery
- **Tier 4:** Certification (£5,997+) - Professional training

**WHY:**
- Crystal clear customer journey
- Each tier has distinct purpose
- Easy to communicate and market

---

### 4. **Funnel-Based Revenue Model**

**BEFORE:**
- Revenue projections based on course sales
- No clear conversion assumptions
- Conservative: £55,720 / Optimistic: £190,130

**AFTER:**
- Funnel-based with conversion rates:
  - 200-500 students in 30-Day Challenge
  - 15-25% conversion to 90-Day Transformation
  - Community and deepening for graduates
- Conservative: £58,010 / Optimistic: £167,881

**WHY:**
- More realistic financial modeling
- Based on actual funnel conversion rates
- Accounts for customer lifetime value
- Sustainable growth model

---

### 5. **Implementation Roadmap Refocused**

**BEFORE:**
- Launch 3 courses simultaneously
- Broad approach across tiers
- No clear MVP

**AFTER:**
- **Months 1-2:** 30-Day Challenge ONLY (validate market)
- **Months 3-4:** 90-Day Transformation (core product)
- **Months 5+:** Expand ecosystem strategically

**WHY:**
- Focus on MVP (minimum viable product)
- Validate market before scaling
- Prove concept with real students
- Reduce development risk

## STRATEGIC BENEFITS

### For Customers:
1. **Lower barrier to entry** (£47 vs £97)
2. **Proof before commitment** (test method in 30 days)
3. **Clear progression path** (Challenge → Transformation → Deepening)
4. **Less decision fatigue** (one clear entry point)

### For Business:
1. **Better conversion funnel** (£47 → £497 upsell)
2. **Qualified leads** (Challenge graduates know method works)
3. **Sustainable growth** (MRR from community + courses)
4. **Scalable model** (certification creates leverage)

### For Marketing:
1. **Simpler messaging** ("Start with £47, transform in 90 days")
2. **Clear value ladder** (each tier solves specific need)
3. **Compelling story arc** (journey from beginner to practitioner)
4. **Easy to explain** (2-step process: Challenge, then Transformation)

## WHAT STAYS THE SAME

The core framework remains unchanged:
- **3 Pillars:** View, Compass, Ground
- **5 Bodies:** Mental, Emotional, Physical, Energetic, Spiritual
- **8 Circles:** Progression from 0 (conceptual) to 8 (embodied wisdom)
- **Course content:** All curriculum stays the same
- **Brand voice:** Authentic, wise, clear, grounded

Only the **positioning and sequencing** changed, not the substance.

## CONCLUSION

The refined strategy creates a **clearer, more profitable, and more sustainable** path forward:

- **30-Day Challenge** = The gateway (low risk, high value)
- **90-Day Transformation** = The flagship (deep transformation, strong economics)
- **Deepening Courses** = Mastery path (recurring revenue, community building)

The customer journey is now:
1. **Discover** (free content, lead magnet)
2. **Test** (30-Day Challenge, £47)
3. **Transform** (90-Day Transformation, £497)
4. **Deepen** (Pillar courses, £497-997)
5. **Master** (Certification, £5,997+)

This is a proven business model for online education with clear metrics, realistic projections, and a path to £300k-500k+ by Year 2.

**The foundation is solid. The strategy is refined. The path is clear.**
