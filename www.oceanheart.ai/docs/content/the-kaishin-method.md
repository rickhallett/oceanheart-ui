## The Kaishin Method: An Integrated Framework for Human Thriving

**The Kaishin Method** is the systematic application of the principles explored in _Ocean Heart, Ocean Mind_. It is a comprehensive path for personal mastery, guiding individuals from foundational understanding to a life of effortless, embodied wisdom.

The Method is built upon **Three Core Pillars** of practice, which are developed and integrated through **Eight Concentric Circles of Mastery**.

### The Three Pillars (The Domains of Practice)

1.  **The View (Zen & Non-Duality):** The practice of seeing what is already true. This is the recognition of your fundamental nature as silent, open Awareness—the source of unshakable peace.
2.  **The Compass (ACT & Psychology):** The science-backed skills for a meaningful life. This is the practice of unhooking from difficult thoughts, clarifying your values, and taking committed action in the world.
3.  **The Ground (Somatic Work):** The practice of coming home to the body. This is the gentle release of stored tension from the nervous system, building the body’s capacity to be a safe and wise foundation for a quiet mind.

### The Eight Circles (The Stages of Mastery)

The Circles represent your progressive journey of deepening and integrating the Three Pillars. You may be at different Circles in different Pillars at any given time; the goal of the Method is to foster balanced development across all three.

### How The System Works Together: The Synergy of the Method

**The Kaishin Method** is not a linear path. It is a spiral of deepening integration.

- **Ground is the Foundation:** A dysregulated nervous system (**Ground**) makes it nearly impossible to hold the subtle insights of **The View** or act effectively with **The Compass**. All progress begins with establishing safety in the body.
- **The View Provides Context:** Without **The View**, the work of **The Compass** (psychology) can become an endless project of self-improvement. The recognition of your inherent wholeness provides the profound context in which psychological healing can gracefully unfold.
- **The Compass Builds the Bridge:** **The View** can be profound, but **The Compass** is what allows you to translate that boundless freedom into a meaningful, effective, and loving life. It bridges the absolute and the relative.

A student of **The Kaishin Method** learns to self-assess where they are in the Circles for each Pillar, identify their "leading edge" of practice, and apply the right tools to foster balanced, holistic, and lasting transformation.
